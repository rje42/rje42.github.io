### Biography

I am a Professor of [Statistical](https://www.stats.ox.ac.uk) Science 
at the [University of Oxford](https://www.ox.ac.uk), and the Kay Fellow in Statistics at [Jesus 
College](https://www.jesus.ox.ac.uk).

I completed my PhD in 2011 from the [University of Washington](https://stat.uw.edu), and was a Postdoctoral 
Research Fellow at the [Statistical Laboratory](https://www.statslab.cam.ac.uk) in 
Cambridge until 2013.
